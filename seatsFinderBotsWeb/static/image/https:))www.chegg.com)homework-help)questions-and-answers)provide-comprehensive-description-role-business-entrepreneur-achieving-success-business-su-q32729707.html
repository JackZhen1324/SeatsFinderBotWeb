Question:

Provide a comprehensive description of the role of a “business entrepreneur” in achieving the success of a business. Be sure to explain how this role differ from that of a “businessman”?

Best Answer:

A business entrepreneur is a person who builds a successful business model around a product or service which is new and attempts to solve a problem which has never been done before. The business entrepreneur is often the person who identifies the existing problem, develops the idea of a solution and then is responsible for translating the idea into a full scaled business with a viable revenue model that successfully solves the problem. The business entrepreneur is also responsible for raising the funds required for setting up the new business, marketing the new concept or idea to potential investors, hiring people to run the business and overall co-ordination for the entire business. Thus ultimately it is the business entrepreneur’s ingenuity and his ability to market the idea that decides the success of the business.

In case of a business man, the person is responsible for running the business and its success or failure but the business is often based on an established idea and is not on a new concept on idea. Thus the fundamental difference between a business entrepreneur and a business man is that business entrepreneur works on a new concept and develops a business model around it. While a business man develops a business but it is not on a novel concept or idea.
